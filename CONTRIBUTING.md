# Contributing to AGORA

AGORA is an early-stage research project. We welcome contributors who care about:

- Agent-based modeling and simulation
- Personal development and human flourishing
- Complexity science applied to social systems
- LLM-powered reasoning systems

## Areas of Contribution

- **Graph schema design** — how to model development journeys as knowledge graphs
- **Simulation rules** — what agent behaviors and decision patterns to encode
- **LLM prompting** — how to get consistent, meaningful agent reasoning per round
- **Report generation** — structured prediction output from simulation results
- **Domain datasets** — longitudinal narrative data from real development communities (anonymized)

## How to Get Involved

Open an issue to discuss before submitting a PR. This is a research project — the design questions matter as much as the code.
